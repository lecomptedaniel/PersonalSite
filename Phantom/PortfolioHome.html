I am Daniel Le Compte, a User Experience Researcher

I am skilled at diving to the root of a problem through deeply understanding humans - then transferring that information into actionable insights for designs and solutions that transform lives.

I am currently a student in the MHCI program at the Human-Computer Interation Institue at Carnegie Mellon University. Previously, I was a student within UW's HCDE certificate program, as well as EWU studying social and cognitive psychology. Projects from these educational experiences are below as well as many others.
I also have worked for Facebook Reality Labs, on contract, on Audio research to redefine human hearing in the context of virual, mixed, and augmented reality. You can read about this work in this article from Facebook.

This page lists various projects that I have been involved in. Each tile or card represents a project, click within to learn more about my involvement and the project itself. Each project has tags for what part of the process I was involved in: Research, Design, or Coding, respectively.
